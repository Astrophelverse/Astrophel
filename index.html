Alcor
Decentralized Voice Data Marketplace

In Ursa Major, Alcor is the faint star hidden behind Mizar. Ancient armies used it as an eyesight test — if you could see Alcor, you were elite.

The AI industry has the same blind spot. Billions of African language speakers are invisible to AI models. Alcor pays locals to contribute verified voice data in Hausa, Swahili, Yoruba and more. Verified on-chain. Encrypted end-to-end. Instant settlement.
